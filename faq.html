FAQs

How to recycle?

+

To recycle your plastic bottle follow these stages.

  • 1. find a nearby recycle station
  • 2. Scan your bottle holding it sideways
  • 3. Hold your bottle on top of the station and the camera above it
  • 4. Make sure the phone camera covers top of the station only and your hand holding the bottle
  • 5. Drop your plastic bottle carefully

Stations

+

Recycle stations can be found on the map, to tell what station you have to use, check if the station number is equal to the one you see on the app.

What can i use with my Cycl Coins?

+

You can spend your Coins on discounts from restaurants, barbershops, donations and much more.
