FAQs

How to recycle?

+

To recycle you bottle, Find a nearby recycle station, then Scan your bottle on vertical position.

  • 1. Find a nearby recycle station
  • 2. Scan your bottle on vertical position
  • 3.Position your bottle on top of the bin
  • have your phone camera over the bottle while holding it with the other hand
  • Drop it and get rewarded

    • Make sure that the camera covers only the black part of the station

Why does the stations have a number?

+

Every recycle station has a unique number. These numbers are used to tell the difference between the stations. You can see the station number on every station under the logo.

What can i use with my Cycl Coins?

+

When you safe enough CC, they can be used to redeem coupons and get give aways.
